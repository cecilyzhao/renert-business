About Us

Bridging Classroom & Business

Calgary has almost no high school business clubs. We're changing that.

Our Mission

To equip students with practical business, investment, and entrepreneurship skills through hands-on projects and collaborative learning.

"We believe business education should be accessible and exciting for every Calgary high school student."

Our Vision

To build a community where students explore business ideas, compete on national and international stages, and develop the confidence to lead.

RBS is brand new — and that means founding members get to shape everything from the ground up.

🎯

Leadership

Develop real leadership skills through events, competitions, and team projects.

💡

Innovation

Turn ideas into action with pitch events, simulated markets, and student-built tools.

🤝

Community

Build connections, help peers succeed, and give back through service projects.

What We Do

Activities & Programs

From trading simulations to business case competitions — there's something for everyone.

📈

Investment Lab

Learn portfolio management through simulated trading. Analyze markets, build strategies, and compete in investment challenges against teams locally and globally.

📋

Case Study & Challenges

Analyze real companies and work on case studies in teams. Take on challenges, present your ideas, and strengthen your business analysis and presentation skills for real-world success.

🚀

Entrepreneurship & Mini Shark Tank

Collaborate in teams to build real-world projects and pitch your business ideas in a Shark Tank-style format. Learn, create, and have fun!

Compete

Competitions

Represent RBS on provincial, national, and international stages.

Wharton Global Investment Competition

September – April

One of the world's premier high school investing competitions, run by the Wharton School at UPenn.

Diamond Challenge

September – January

An international entrepreneurship competition for high school students hosted by the University of Delaware.

FBLA — Future Business Leaders of Canada

Year-round

Canada's premier business student organization with provincial and national competitions across multiple events.

School Investment Competition

Ongoing

Our internal simulated trading competition. Build your portfolio and see how you stack up against other RBS members.
